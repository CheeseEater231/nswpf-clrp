Our Mission & Values

The New South Wales Police Force is committed to serving the community with integrity, professionalism, and accountability. We work collaboratively with communities to prevent crime, respond to incidents, and maintain public safety across the state.

Our core values include integrity, respect, innovation, professionalism, and service. We aim to build trust, maintain high standards of policing, and support a safe and secure environment for all citizens.
